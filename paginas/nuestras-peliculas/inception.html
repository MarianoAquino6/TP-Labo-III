INCEPTION





SINOPSIS

Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a skilled thief who specializes in extracting valuable information by entering people's dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to clear his criminal record by performing an impossible task: "inception," the act of planting an idea into the mind of a C.E.O. through his dreams. However, the risk is high, as the subconscious mind can be a dangerous landscape. Cobb assembles a team of specialists to pull off the heist, but their biggest challenge may be Cobb himself and the ghosts of his past.
